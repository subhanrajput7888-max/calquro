How Age is Calculated

Age calculation considers the exact date and time differences between your birth date and the current date (or specified date).

  • Years: Complete years lived
  • Months: Additional complete months
  • Days: Additional complete days
  • Hours: Additional complete hours
  • Minutes: Additional complete minutes
  • Seconds: Additional complete seconds

Uses of Age Calculator

  • Find exact age for official documents
  • Calculate age for insurance applications
  • Determine eligibility for age-restricted activities
  • Plan birthday celebrations and milestones
  • Calculate age differences between people
  • Academic and research purposes
  • Historical age calculations
